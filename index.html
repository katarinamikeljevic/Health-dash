🔵 Fat — how to split it
Keep fat low in the morning (kefir shake + breakfast = max 18g combined). Avocado goes at lunch — it slows digestion and prevents the 4pm crash. Dinner gets the remainder — fish has natural fat, cottage cheese is very low. Don't combine multiple fat sources in one meal.
🟤 Fibre — how to split it
Front-load fibre at lunch — lentils, chickpeas, edamame, peas and avocado give you 12–15g in one meal. Kefir shake adds ~3g (flax + chia). Breakfast adds ~2–4g. Aim to hit 28g by end of lunch so dinner just needs a small top-up from veg. No need to force fibre at dinner — it can cause bloating overnight.
